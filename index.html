White River Junction, Vermont

This little town is nestled between two rivers and rolling hills. White River Junction is experiencing a cultural revival - with artists and small businesses injecting life into this old railroad village. It's central location in the Upper Valley make it a perfect jumping off point for Adventures.

Getting Here

The train makes for a gorgeous ride and easy access to everything White River has to offer.

  • Vermonter

    This train runs daily from Washington, DC through Philadelphia, New York City and to WRJ and beyond to Northern Vermont.

  • Driving

    4.5 Hours from NYC up I-91
    and only 2 hours from Boston up I-89

  • Bus

    Checkout Greyhound or the Dartmouth Coach for buses into the area.
